# Register Matrix

## Recipient Posture

| Recipient Class | Register | Objective | Avoid |
|---|---|---|---|
| Regulator or statutory authority | Statutory authority | Establish compliance frame, evidence, and requested action | Deference, apology, emotional over-explanation |
| ADA coordinator or institutional compliance office | Rights-preserving precision | Identify duty, barrier, requested modification, and deadline | Gratitude padding, vague hardship language |
| Opposing party or adversarial actor | Adversarial precision | Narrow the issue, preserve record, prevent mischaracterization | Conciliatory hedging, over-disclosure |
| Professional peer | Collegial directness | Move work forward with specific asks and evidence | Performative warmth, diffuse context |
| Personal contact | Natural voice | Preserve relational context without artificial formality | Corporate tone, legalistic inflation |
| Vendor or service provider | Transactional specificity | State account, defect, remedy, documentation, deadline | Unnecessary autobiography |

## Tone Corrections

| User Correction | Required Adjustment |
|---|---|
| Too polite | Remove gratitude padding, apologies, and permissive phrasing. |
| Uneducated | Elevate diction, specificity, and procedural command. |
| Boilerplate | Remove generic templates and make the text case-specific. |
| Too soft | Strengthen verbs, deadlines, and accountability language. |
| Too much | Compress to objective, evidence, demand, and deadline. |
