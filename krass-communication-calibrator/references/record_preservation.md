# Record Preservation

## Use

Apply when drafting to regulators, institutions, providers, opposing parties, vendors, or any actor likely to dispute facts later.

## Preservation Fields

| Field | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `event_date` | Anchor the factual timeline. |
| `actor` | Identify who acted or failed to act. |
| `document_or_exhibit` | Link the claim to an artifact. |
| `source_ledger_id` | Connect the claim to provenance. |
| `requested_action` | State the remedy or next step. |
| `deadline` | Prevent open-ended drift. |
| `reservation` | Preserve rights without overclaiming. |

## Drafting Rules

- Correct the record before escalating demands.
- Use neutral verbs for facts and firm verbs for requested action.
- Avoid accusations that exceed the evidence grade.
- Request preservation of records when communications, logs, recordings, medical records, account notes, or access records may become material.
