# Recipient Posture

## Communication Mapping

| Recipient | Posture | Structure | Evidence Handling |
|---|---|---|---|
| Regulator | Statutory authority | Jurisdiction, violation or issue, evidence, requested action, deadline | Cite verified statutes, rules, dates, documents |
| ADA coordinator | Rights-preserving precision | Barrier, limitation, requested modification, interactive-process demand, deadline | Cite policy or law only after verification |
| Opposing party | Adversarial precision | Record correction, disputed conduct, demand, preservation language | Use concise exhibits and avoid over-disclosure |
| Professional peer | Collegial directness | Context, decision, ask, deadline | Include only decision-relevant evidence |
| Personal contact | Natural voice | Direct message, personal context, ask | Avoid institutional register |
| Vendor/provider | Transactional specificity | Account or service, issue, evidence, remedy, deadline | Attach or cite order, date, policy, or record |

## Default Closing Postures

| Context | Closing |
|---|---|
| Compliance or rights matter | State deadline and expected action. |
| Professional coordination | State next step and owner. |
| Adversarial exchange | Preserve record and avoid unnecessary concessions. |
| Personal note | End naturally without corporate phrasing. |
